Return Policy

REFUNDS

Unfortunately, we do not allow refunds for the one-time joining fee that you pay when you sign up.

However, you can cancel your recurring monthly subscription at any time by sending an email to support@betterbeats.io requesting a cancellation.

Make sure to use the email address you signed up with when submitting a cancellation request.

Cancellations will be approved with 24 hours. When your cancellation request is approved, you will retain access to Better Beats University for the remainder of your last payment period. For example, if you paid for the month of July, you would retain access until August 1.


EUROPEAN UNION 14 DAY COOLING OFF PERIOD

Notwithstanding the above, if merchandise is being shipped into the European Union, you have the right to cancel or refund your order within 14 days for any reason and without justification. You’ll need the receipt or proof of purchase.
