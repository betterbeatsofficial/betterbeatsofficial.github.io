Return Policy

REFUNDS

We have a 30-day refund policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to request a refund.

To request a refund, you can contact us at support@betterbeats.io.

If approved, you’ll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days. Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund too.

If more than 15 business days have passed since we’ve approved your return, please contact us at support@betterbeats.io.You can always contact us for any refund questions at support@betterbeats.io.
